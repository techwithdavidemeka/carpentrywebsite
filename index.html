who we are

Chartered Carpentry is a furniture production company in Nigeria that provides carpentry services to property developers in the real estate industry, government agencies, corporate organizations as well as individuals in Nigeria.

We are a team of highly skilled people, working with the best technology in furniture production to provide creative and innovative designs in carpentry, furniture making and cabinetry.
